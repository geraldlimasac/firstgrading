FRIED CHICKEN


These fried chickens can be described as being "crunchy","juicy" and as well as "crispy".
This dish has alsi been called "spicy" and "salty". The saltiness of the fried chicken invites more saliva
to produced, allowing to break down the crispy skin more and ease your digestive processing. Fried chicken
makes you drool over it by just smelling and imagining the taste of it. Fried chicken is very tasty treat
that is sure to relieve your hunger.

INGREDIENTS

  • Chickens legs
  • Chicken breast
  • Chicken wings
  • Breading mix

PREPARATION

  • Chicken parts
  • Breading mix
  • Mixing bowl
  • Cooking oil
  • Frying pan
  • Tongs
  • strainer colander

PROCEDURE

  1. Put cooking oil into the frying pan just the amount to fry and heat the pan with the fire from the stove
  2. Wash the chicken by water in the strainer
  3. Put the chicken in the mixing bowl together with the breading mix
  4. Mix the chicken and the breading mix thoroughly
  5. Put the chicken one by one in the frying pan using the tongs
  6. Wait and check until the chicken is golden brown
  7. By using the tongs, get the chicken and put on the serving plate
  8. Ready to serve
