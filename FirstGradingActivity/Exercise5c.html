BANANACUE


Bananacue is made with deep fried bananas coated in caramelized brown suger. The bananas used for this recipe are "Saba bananas",which are very commonly used
for cooking here in the Philippines. It is usually skewered on a bamboo stick, and sold on the streets. This bananacue sure is one of the old and traditional food in the Philippines yet surely one of the best.
Bananacue can surely satisfy your hunger.

INGREDIENTS

  • Banana
  • Sugar
  • Cooking oil

PREPARATION

  • peeled banana
  • Sugar
  • Cooking oil
  • Frying pan
  • Tongs

PROCEDURE

  1. Put cooking oil into the frying pan just the amount to deep fry and heat the pan with the fire from the stove. Until the cooking oil is hot
  2. Put the banana in the pan along with the sugar
  3. steer the banana using the tongs until the sugar is caramelized and be coated on the banana
  4. When you saw the banana is golden brown you can get it using the tongs and put it on the serving plate
  5. Ready to serve
